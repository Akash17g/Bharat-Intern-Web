General Knowledge regarding Celsius and Fahrenheit

Celsius: The Celsius scale, also known as the Centigrade scale, was developed by Swedish astronomer Anders Celsius in 1742.

Fahrenheit: The Fahrenheit scale was created by German-Polish physicist Daniel Gabriel Fahrenheit in 1724.
